The base spacing unit is `4px`

For example,

- `p-1` is `padding: 4px` (1 × spacing unit)
- `m-8` is `margin: 32px` (8 × spacing unit)
- `w-256` is `width: 1024px` (256 × spacing unit)
- `min-h-192` is `min-height: 768px` (192 × spacing unit)
